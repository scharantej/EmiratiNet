Traditional Customs

Dubai's traditional customs are deeply rooted in the city's Islamic heritage. These customs include:

  • Respect for elders and family members
  • Hospitality and generosity
  • Modesty in dress and behavior
  • Respect for the environment
  • Honesty and integrity

Local Arts and Crafts

Dubai has a rich tradition of arts and crafts. These crafts include:

  • Pottery
  • Weaving
  • Jewelry making
  • Calligraphy
  • Woodworking

Regional Cuisine

Dubai's regional cuisine is a blend of traditional Arabic and international flavors. Some of the most popular dishes include:

  • Machboos
  • Kebabs
  • Hummus
  • Falafel
  • Baklava

Famous Personalities

Dubai has produced a number of famous personalities, including:

  • Sheikh Mohammed bin Rashid Al Maktoum
  • Khalifa bin Zayed Al Nahyan
  • Abdulla bin Zayed Al Nahyan
  • Sultan bin Sulayem
  • Ahlam

Key Events

Dubai has hosted a number of key events, including:

  • Expo 2020
  • The Dubai World Cup
  • The Dubai Airshow
  • The Dubai International Film Festival
  • The Dubai Jazz Festival
