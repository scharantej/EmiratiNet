Welcome to Dubai!

Dubai is a city with a rich and fascinating history. From its humble beginnings as a small fishing village to its current status as a global metropolis, Dubai has undergone a remarkable transformation.

This website will take you on a journey through Dubai's past, from its early settlement to its modern-day development. You'll learn about the people, places, and events that have shaped this dynamic city.

Historical Landmarks

  • Burj Khalifa
  • Dubai Creek
  • Jumeirah Mosque
  • Al Fahidi Fort
  • Dubai Museum

Historical Events

  • Foundation of Dubai (1833)
  • Discovery of oil (1966)
  • Formation of the United Arab Emirates (1971)
  • Construction of the Burj Khalifa (2010)
  • Hosting of Expo 2020 (2020)
